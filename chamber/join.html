Gold Membership Level

Cost: $100

Benefits: Gain priority access to exclusive business events, premium networking opportunities, and enhanced support tailored to your growth.

Training: Receive specialized training sessions designed to improve your team’s expertise and business operations.

Advertising: Get featured placements on our homepage, social media, and newsletters to maximize your brand's reach.

Silver Membership Level

Cost: $50

Benefits: Access valuable networking events, business resources, and standard support services.

Training: Participate in group training sessions and webinars to enhance your business skills.

Advertising: Get a featured listing in our business directory and occasional promotions on our platform.

Bronze Membership Level

Cost: $25

Benefits: Gain access to essential business resources and entry-level networking opportunities.

Training: Receive access to basic training materials and self-paced online courses.

Advertising: Get listed in our business directory with limited promotional features.

Non-Profile Membership Level

Cost: Free

Benefits: Limited access to public networking events and community forums.

Training: Access to selected free resources and introductory business guides.

Advertising: No advertising benefits, but you can upgrade anytime for more exposure.
